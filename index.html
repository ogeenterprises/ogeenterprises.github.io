What we do

Chain Conveyor Systems

Chain conveyor is used for moving empty and filled cylinders from one location in the plant to another location, passing through various processing points. This facilitates cylinder movement and automation in production.. 'Dry Type' system can also be provided.

PLC Based Purging Unit

This equipment is used for removal of vacuum from newly supplied/re-qualified empty cylinders. A small quantity of Vapour is filled into these cylinders. This process is essential for new cylinders/re-qualified which are being filled for the first time. This eliminates the possibility of retaining an explosive LPG – Air mixture in the cylinder. This equipment can also be offered in a fully PLC Based Automation version .
